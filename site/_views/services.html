Professional Installation

Every house is different, which we love! The steps for a new installation versus a renovation is quite different. Your personal taste and flooring choice will also drive how we proceed but here  is an idea of our general process.

1. On-Site Design Meeting

We laser measure your home to map out the installation of your floor and collaborate with you on your wants, needs, and preferences. There are many options available and we will walk you through each step to help decide what will be best for your unique home.

2. Floor Preparation

In this step we address the quality of your subfloor, this will affect the final result of your hardwood flooring. To do this we inspect the existing squeaks and creaks in the floor and ensure  the subfloor has the proper moisture level. We want to know if the floor is flat, smooth, and in good condition.

3. Layout

We then make the flooring to fit your home, taking into consideration the shape of your unique space. Proper layout from the start ensures a flawless finish.

4. Installation

This step will differ depending on what type of subfloor and flooring you choose. Some installations require underlay, cleats, adhesive, or a combination. We always source high-quality products to do our jobs. We pay attention to detail and treat your floor as if it were our own.
