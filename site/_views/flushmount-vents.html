Flushmount Vents

Flush mount vents are designed and built to sit into your hardwood floors. Positioned “flush” to the floor means there is no rim or lip around them. This results in vents that will not limit furniture placement and have a seamless appearance.

We construct the vents locally which means they are custom made to your specifications. This style of vents can be used for heating/cooling air vents, cold air returns, or access panels.
