Staircases and Recaps

Now that you’ve upgraded your floor, you may want to improve the appearance of your staircases because they often are the focal point of your home. Recapping your stairs can refresh its appearance in a way that is much less disruptive than a new build there fore requiring very little demolition.

Every house is unique, so we will visit your house to plan the optimal custom installation for you.

Carpeted Stairs

We remove the carpet. We then install a new riser and tread to match the wood color you already have in your home.

Existing Hardwood Stairs

We sand the stairs. We then stain the stairs to match your existing hardwood floor or your desired color.

New Build

We design and build the stairs exactly to your needs. We eliminate unnecessary construction dust and debris by building and finishing the stairs in our local shop. We install them in your home and your stairs are then ready to use without delay.
